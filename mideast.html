Sopa Paraguaia

In the central-west region of the country there is also a lot of indigenous influence in the cuisine, both from the Brazilian Indians themselves and from our Paraguayan neighbors. However, this region of great mixtures of peoples also has a very varied cuisine, with influences from various parts of Brazil. You eat a lot of fish, but also a lot of red meat and even some exotic proteins. A popular dish in the region is the Paraguayan soup, which is not actually a soup, but a savory cake that uses corn flour as a base and is very popular in the region.
