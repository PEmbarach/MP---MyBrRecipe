Arroz Carreteiro

There is much more to the cuisine of the southern region of the country than the traditional barbecue. Among so many typical southern dishes, Carreteiro rice stands out, one of the favorites of the gauchos. The dish is part of the history of Rio Grande do Sul and emerged when the main means of transporting loads were carts pulled by oxen. The “carreteiros” of the time created this simple dish, which takes few ingredients: beef jerky, a meat that could be preserved during several days of travel, combined with rice, defines the base of the dish. Today, it is common to make Carreteiro rice with beef

How to make

  1. Cut the dried meat into cubes and boil 2 times, changing the water each time it boils to remove excess salt.
  2. Fry the bacon until golden and set aside, fry the sausages and set aside.
  3. Then put the garlic and then the onion and let it sauté until it's done.
  4. Then add the sausage, jerky and bacon, let it cook for a while, then add the rice.
  5. Add enough boiling water and let it dry until ready to serve.
